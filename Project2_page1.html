About Us

Our History

Welcome to Michael and Michelle’s March Madness, where passion for basketball meets exceptional products and services! Our journey began in 2005 when childhood friends Michael and Michelle, both avid basketball enthusiasts, decided to turn their shared love for the sport into a business venture. Starting out as a small online store selling basketball merchandise, the company quickly gained traction within the basketball community due to its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

As the years passed, Michael and Michelle’s March Madness expanded its offerings to include a wide range of basketball-related products, from apparel and footwear to accessories and equipment. Our dedication to providing top-notch products led to partnerships with leading brands in the industry, ensuring that our customers have access to the latest and greatest in basketball gear. We believe that great players need the highest quality products to support their game and avoid injuries.

But we didn’t stop there. Recognizing the growing demand for basketball-related services, we diversified our business to include coaching clinics, training camps, and event management services. Our team of experienced coaches and event planners are dedicated to helping basketball enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels reach their full potential on and off the court.

Our Mission

At Michael and Michelle’s March Madness, our mission is to ignite and nurture a lifelong passion for basketball in every individual we reach. Through our dedication to offering the highest quality basketball products and services, we commit to empowering athletes of all ages and skill levels to excel on and off the court. We strive to be more than a brand; we aim to be a community pillar that supports, inspires, and unites basketball enthusiasts around the globe. Our goal is to create an environment where the thrill of March Madness can be experienced all year round, fostering growth, excellence, and the pure joy of the game.

From their humble beginnings as childhood friends who loved the sport, to their decision to create a business that revolves around basketball, their passion fuels their dedication to providing the best products and services for like-minded enthusiasts.

This ensures that customers not only receive the best in basketball gear but also trust in the durability and performance of their purchases.
